# Feedback & Support

We want Magidesk to be the best POS for your business. Your feedback drives our development!

## Reporting Bugs
If something isn't working right:

1. **Take a Picture**: Use your phone to snap a photo of the error message or screen.
2. **Note the Steps**: Write down what you were doing just before the error happened (e.g., "I pressed Split Ticket and then selected Table 5").
3. **Contact Support**: Email us at `support@magidesk.com` with the details.

## Requesting Features
